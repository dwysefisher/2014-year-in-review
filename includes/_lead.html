LEAD.

Leadership has been a central precept of the Eureka College culture since our inception in 1855. Leadership education incorporated into student learning is foundational to the Eureka experience. Similarly, we believe our institution plays a significant leadership role in the community, region and nation and we work to ensure that decisions made by our Administration and Board of Trustees provide continued leadership to the industry of higher education.
